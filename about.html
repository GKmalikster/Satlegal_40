FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is SatLegal free to use?+
Yes. The core 5-step legal analysis — including law detection, document checklist, and preliminary case readiness score — is completely free for all users. Premium features like full report PDF, lawyer connect, and case tracking require a free account.
Is this legal advice?+
No. SatLegal provides legal information and analysis for educational purposes only. Our AI helps you understand which laws may apply, what documents you need, and your preliminary probability — but this is not a substitute for professional legal advice from a qualified advocate. Always consult a registered lawyer before taking legal action.
How accurate is the law detection?+
Our detection engine covers 37 Indian laws with thousands of keywords, section references, Hinglish phrases, and exact legal phrases. For common legal situations (divorce, cheque bounce, cyber fraud, property disputes), accuracy is very high. We always recommend verifying the specific applicable sections with a qualified lawyer.
Is my case information private?+
Absolutely. Your case description and analysis are encrypted and stored only in your account. We never share your information with third parties, lawyers, or advertisers without your explicit consent. The law detection itself runs entirely on your device — no data is sent to any server during the analysis phase.
Which laws does SatLegal cover?+
SatLegal covers 37 major Indian laws across Family, Property, Criminal, Employment, Cyber, Consumer, Civil, Succession, Tax, Constitutional, NRI, and IP categories. This includes the new BNS 2023 (replacing IPC), BNSS 2023 (replacing CrPC), Muslim Women (Protection of Rights on Marriage) Act 2019, NDPS Act, POCSO Act, and RFCTLARR 2013 (Land Acquisition).
How do I connect with a lawyer?+
After completing your analysis and creating a free account, you can browse and connect with verified lawyers on our platform who specialise in your specific legal area. Every lawyer is bar-registered and manually verified before being listed.

⚠️ Legal Disclaimer: SatLegal provides legal information for general educational purposes only. This is not a legal advice platform. Laws are dynamic and change and their application solely depends on the specific facts put into the query box. Always consult a qualified lawyer before taking any legal action. SatLegal is an information platform only — and not a law firm.
