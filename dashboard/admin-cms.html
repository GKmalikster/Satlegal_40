🔒 Access Controls — User Journey Gates

Control which steps in the user flow are mandatory. These settings affect what users see on the platform in real-time.

Payment Required for Report
When ON: users must pay ₹499 to access their legal analysis report. When OFF: reports are generated for free.
Signup Required Before Report
When ON: users must register/login before viewing their analysis. When OFF: anonymous access is allowed — no account needed.
Free Report Access (Override / Bypass Payment)
Master override: grants all users free report access regardless of the Payment Required setting above. Useful for demo mode, launch period, or A/B testing.
Consultation Booking Enabled
Show the "Book a Lawyer Consultation" option on reports and analysis pages. Disable to hide this feature entirely.
⚡ Feature Toggles
Law Firm Assignment
Show matched law firms to users after their legal analysis is complete. Disable to hide the law firm recommendation section.
AI Legal Chatbot
Enable the AI legal assistant chatbot widget on the platform. When OFF, the chatbot button and panel are hidden from all pages.
Show Confidence Score to Users
Display law match confidence percentages in the analysis report. When OFF, only law names and summaries are shown without scores.
Show Disclaimer Banner
Display the legal disclaimer banner at the top of analysis results. Recommended ON for compliance. Disabling removes the disclaimer from user-facing pages.
