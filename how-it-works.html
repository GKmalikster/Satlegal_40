Common Questions

Is this a substitute for a lawyer? ›
No. SatLegal is an educational and informational tool. It helps you understand which laws apply to your situation and how strong your case may be, so you're better prepared before meeting a lawyer. It does not constitute legal advice and does not create a lawyer-client relationship.
Is my information private and secure? ›
Yes. Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We never share your case details with third parties. Your analysis is private to you.
Can I write in Hindi or Hinglish? ›
Yes. Our engine understands Hindi, Hinglish (Hindi written in Roman script), and English. You can write in any combination, e.g. "mere landlord ne deposit return nahi kiya 6 mahine se."
How accurate is the law mapping? ›
Our law engine covers 78 major Indian laws with thousands of keyword patterns. It achieves over 99% accuracy on our regression test suite of 1,000 scenarios. However, edge cases and highly specific situations may benefit from additional lawyer review.
What if I'm not satisfied with the report? ›
If the report doesn't match your situation or if we couldn't identify applicable laws, contact us at support@satlegal.in for a review. Refunds are handled case by case within 48 hours.
How is the ₹2,500 consultation conducted? ›
After payment, you'll be asked to book a time slot. A verified Indian lawyer matched to your case type will call you at your chosen time for a 30-minute session. They will also draft a legal notice if needed as part of the consultation.
